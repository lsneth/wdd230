Our Story

Bountiful Foods was founded in 2010 by John and Jane Doe. They wanted to create a business that provided fresh and healthy food options to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas.

The company started small with a storefront in Carlsbad, California, but soon expanded to include food trucks that covered the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. Bountiful Foods sources its fruits and produce directly from farmers in the area, as well as from national and international suppliers.

Today, Bountiful Foods is a thriving business that continues to support healthy eating and organic bounty.
