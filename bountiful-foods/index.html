Based in Southern California

The southern California coast is renowned for its thriving agricultural industry, which produces a diverse range of fresh produce, including avocados, strawberries, oranges, and tomatoes. The region's fertile soil, favorable climate, and innovative farming practices make it one of the most productive agricultural regions in the world. Visitors can enjoy locally-grown produce at farmers' markets and farm-to-table restaurants, as well as tours and tastings at vineyards.
