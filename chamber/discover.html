Nestled in the heart of eastern Idaho, Rexburg is a vibrant and welcoming community that is known for its friendly locals, scenic beauty, and small-town charm. Home to Brigham Young University-Idaho, Rexburg boasts a bustling college town atmosphere with a variety of cultural events, art exhibits, and lively music scene. Visitors can explore a number of local landmarks, including the historic Rexburg Tabernacle, the vibrant downtown district, and the nearby Yellowstone Bear World. With a rich pioneer history, the city offers a unique blend of traditional and modern, with a variety of shops, restaurants, and cafes to enjoy. Whether you are here for a quick visit or looking to stay awhile, Rexburg is the perfect destination for anyone seeking a peaceful, family-friendly environment with plenty of small-town charm.

Rexburg is a gateway to some of the most spectacular outdoor adventures in the region. The area boasts an abundance of natural beauty, from the sweeping vistas of the Teton Mountains to the tranquil waters of the Snake River. Outdoor enthusiasts can enjoy a variety of activities, including hiking, biking, fishing, camping, skiing, snowmobiling, and more. With over 60 parks and green spaces, Rexburg provides ample opportunities for picnicking, leisurely strolls, and family fun. Whether you are a seasoned adventurer or simply looking for a peaceful escape from the city, Rexburg's great outdoors has something for everyone to enjoy.

The Rexburg Idaho Temple is a stunning architectural masterpiece and an iconic landmark of the city. Completed in 2008, the temple features a blend of modern and traditional designs, with a stunning white marble exterior and intricate details that reflect the rich pioneer heritage of the area. The temple is a sacred place of worship for members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, who come from all over the world to visit and participate in spiritual ordinances. Surrounded by meticulously maintained gardens and lush greenery, the temple provides a peaceful and serene environment for meditation, reflection, and prayer. Visitors of all faiths are welcome to enjoy the temple grounds and admire the beauty of this magnificent structure, which stands as a testament to the faith and dedication of the community.

Brigham Young University-Idaho (BYU-Idaho) is a thriving four-year university located in the heart of Rexburg, Idaho. The university is part of the Brigham Young University system and is dedicated to providing a quality education that integrates both spiritual and secular learning. With over 34,000 students enrolled, BYU-Idaho offers a wide range of academic programs, including business, education, health, humanities, and social sciences. The campus is known for its state-of-the-art facilities, innovative technology, and commitment to student success. Students can take advantage of a variety of resources, including research opportunities, internships, and extracurricular activities, as well as a vibrant and welcoming community of peers and faculty. Whether you are looking to start your academic journey or further your career, BYU-Idaho offers a dynamic and supportive environment for students of all backgrounds and interests.

Rexburg's iconic water tower is a sight to behold - a towering beacon that can be seen from miles away, and an unmistakable landmark that is the pride of the city. The water tower stands as a testament to the ingenuity and engineering prowess of the community, and to the enduring importance of hydration. It's so tall that some people joke it's the only way to get a decent cell phone signal in the area. Others claim that it's the tallest structure in the city, although no one seems to know for sure. Despite its status as a water tower, some locals have been known to refer to it as the "world's largest sippy cup." Whether you're a local or just passing through, the water tower is a must-see attraction that is sure to make you smile - if not for its comical reputation, then for its sheer impressive size!
