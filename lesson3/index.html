Rexburg Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Title

Rexburg Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Rexburg Chamber of Commerce website is to provide information and resources to the local business community, while promoting and supporting the economic growth and development of the Rexburg area. The site will include historical information about the town, current deomgraphics, a directory of local business organizations, a place to become one of our members, and a contact page. Additionally, the website will serve as a resource for residents and visitors to learn about the vibrant business community in Rexburg and all that the town has to offer.
