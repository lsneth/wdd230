Rexburg Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Target Audience

Local business owners and entrepreneurs

The chamber serves as a resource for these individuals and provides opportunities for networking, promoting their business and advocacy.

Community members

The chamber website can serve as a source of information for residents on events, businesses and resources within the community.

Visitors and tourists

The website can provide information on local attractions and businesses for those visiting the area, encouraging them to explore and support the local economy.

Investors and potential business owners

The website can showcase the community's strengths and opportunities for business, encouraging potential investors and entrepreneurs to consider setting up shop in the area.

Local government and civic leaders

The website can provide information on the chamber's role in community development and advocacy, and serve as a resource for these leaders in their decision-making.

Job seekers

The website can provide information on job opportunities and career resources in the area.

Students and recent graduates

The website can provide information on internships and job opportunities for students and recent graduates in the area.

Personas

Persona 1: Small Business Owner

Fictional Name: Tom Smith

Job Title: Owner and Manager of Smith's Hardware Store

Responsibilities: Managing the day-to-day operations of the store, ordering inventory, and working with suppliers

Demographics: Male, 35 years old, Married, College educated, White

Goals and Tasks: Network with other local business owners, discover new opportunities for his business, learn about upcoming events and promotions

Physical, Social and Technological environment: Tom has a small office in his store where he keeps his computer and manages his business, He use his computer and phone to check emails and social media, He is active on social media, He has a small group of friends and family that he works with.

Quote: "I want to make sure my business stays competitive and relevant in the community, and the Chamber's website is a great resource for that."


Persona 2: Job Seeker

Fictional Name: Sarah Johnson

Job Title: Recent college graduate

Responsibilities: Seeking employment in her field of study

Demographics: Female, 22 years old, Single, College educated, African-American

Goals and Tasks: Learn about job opportunities in the area, research companies and industries, and connect with potential employers

Physical, Social and Technological environment: Sarah lives in an apartment with roommates, She use her computer and phone to check emails, job boards and social media, She is active on LinkedIn, She has a small group of friends and family that she works with.

Quote: "I want to find a job that aligns with my skills and interests, and the Chamber's website is a great place to start my search."

Scenarios

A local business owner is looking to network with other businesses in the community and wants to find out about upcoming events and networking opportunities.

A job seeker wants to learn about job opportunities in the area and wants to research companies and industries in the community.

A resident wants to find out about local events and attractions happening in the town.

A visitor wants to learn about local businesses and restaurants in the area to support while they are visiting the town.

A potential investor wants to learn about the business climate and opportunities in the community to decide if it's a good place to set up a new business.
